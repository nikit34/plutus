FAQ

Good questions

Do my subscribers need to leave Telegram to buy?
Yes — the checkout opens in their browser. After payment, they get the delivery page with their content. No new accounts to create, no app to install.
What if my audience is in a country where cards are hard?
Every product page shows both card and crypto checkout. Crypto (USDT-TRC20, BTC, ETH) works everywhere. Typical Telegram audiences see 15–25% of buyers take the crypto route.
Can I send buyers a Telegram invite as delivery?
Yes. Pick "Text" delivery type and paste the invite link in the content. Buyers see it after payment. Perfect for paid private channels or cohort spaces.
Will buyers get email receipts?
Yes, automatically. Plus, you get notified by email on every sale. Email is optional for buyers — they can still complete purchase without leaving their address.
How is this different from a Telegram payment bot?
Bots are powerful but slow to build and maintain. Plutus ships the infrastructure — product page, checkout, delivery, analytics — as one link. No code, no bot token, no payment provider integration.
Can I sell in rubles / euros / my currency?
Stripe card checkout supports 30+ currencies; Plutus converts automatically. Crypto settles in USD-equivalent (USDT). If your audience expects to pay in their local currency, card checkout handles it seamlessly.

How do buyers receive the Notion template?
They get a secure download link that contains the Notion duplicate URL and any companion files. The link is tied to their purchase — buyers cannot share it publicly because it expires and is per-buyer.
Can I sell templates in multiple currencies?
Yes. Card checkout via Stripe supports 30+ currencies — Plutus converts on your behalf. Crypto checkout settles in USD-equivalent (USDT/USDC) so payouts are predictable.
What if my buyer is outside the US or EU?
Card checkout works globally where Stripe is available. For regions where cards fail or buyers prefer it, the crypto button accepts USDT, BTC, ETH, USDC — a huge chunk of international template buyers prefer it.

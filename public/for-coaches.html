Pricing psychology for coaching

How to price coaching products without leaving money on the table

Anchor with three tiers. A standalone $149 program seems expensive; a $149 program next to a $499 package looks like a deal. Most buyers take the middle.

Lead with a cheap diagnostic. A $19 "is this right for you?" workbook warms up buyers and filters out non-fits. Conversion on the $499 offer doubles when preceded by the $19 entry point.

Increase prices quarterly. Your skill compounds. Your prices should too. Plutus shows when your conversion is high enough to raise prices without hurting volume — usually at 3%+ conversion and $100+ revenue-per-view.

FAQ

Good questions

Can I sell 1-on-1 sessions?
Yes, as a product. Use "Link" delivery and paste your Calendly or Cal.com URL. Buyer pays, receives the booking link, schedules. Your availability stays in one place.
How do I sell a multi-week program?
Upload the curriculum as a PDF or a Notion/Teachable link. For live cohorts, set the product as a "Text" delivery with cohort details (start date, group invite, calendar).
What about recurring subscriptions for membership programs?
Plutus is one-time-payment focused today. For recurring memberships, pair it with Circle/Mighty Networks using Plutus to sell the "annual pass" as a one-time product.
Is it compliant with therapy / HIPAA?
Plutus handles product sale and delivery only. Session content, client records, and PHI stay in your dedicated practice software. Use Plutus for product sales; keep clinical records separate.
Can international clients pay?
Yes — Stripe card supports 30+ currencies. Crypto (USDT, BTC, ETH) works globally. Clients in regions where cards are declined can switch to crypto at the checkout screen.
Do I need a website?
No. Your product page is the landing page. Most coaches run their business from a single bio link pointing to Plutus.
